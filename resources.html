Ask Mountain View
The City offers an online way to enable citizens to quickly and easily email staff with questions, concerns and compliments 24-hours-a-day, 7-days-a-week. The City’s Ask Mountain View system provides a list of topic area designations so questions can be routed to the correct City department and staff member, helping to ensure a speedy reply during regular business hours.

Caltrans
Because El Camino Real is a state road, Mountain View has no juristiction over it. For issues regarding El Camino Real and Freeways, contact Caltrans here.
