Submit a Letter to the Editor to the Local Newspapers

For the Mountain View Voice
All views must include a home address and contact phone number. Published letter will also appear on the website, www.MountainViewOnline.com, and occasionally on the Town Square forum. Email your views to letters@MV-Voice.com. Indicate if letter is to be published. Or snail-mail them to: Mountain View Voice, P.O. Box 405, Mountain View, CA 94042. 350 words max.

For the Mercury News
How to submit a letter or opinion column to the Mercury News

For the Daily Post
Submit letters at letters@padailypost.com. Please limit letters to 250 words or less. Shorter letters are printed first and edited least. Include your first and last name along with your address and phone number. Of course your address and phone number will not be printed.

For the Los Altos Town Crier
Submit letters at bruceb@latc.com. Please limit letters to 200 words or less.
